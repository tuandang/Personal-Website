Traveling



I like to travel to places mentioned in my favorite songs, poems, and historical stories. These are the places I have read about from poems, stories, or history when I was young, but never had a chance to experience them in reality. Everytime I come back to Vietnam in the summer, I take the bus to the main city close by, rent a motel and a motorbike, and ride it to these places.

Some of them are the Vi Da village from the famous "This Is Vy Da Village" of Han Mac Tu , the Tran Quoc pagoda in the Vietnamese traditional poem, or the Nam Giao Esplanade where the old kings of Vietnam used to pray for national peace. All the locations are pinned in this map.

By actually going to those places, I can understand those songs, poems, and hitorical stories more deeply, at a more personal level. I always feel intrigued about how the places I go to used to witness many political events, social changes in the long but complicated history of Vietnam.
